Oklahoma Fried Onion

The Oklahoma Fried Onion Burger is a "smashed burger" that involves flattening the patty and placing a large amount of thinly sliced onions on top. As the onions cook, they fuse with the patty, creating a deliciously crispy and caramelized crust. The high heat of the cooking process can even cause some parts of the onions to burn, adding even more flavor to the burger. Adding cheese to the burger takes the flavor to the next level. The Oklahoma burger is easy to make and bursting with flavor that will leave you wanting more.
